Our Boards Public Responsibilities Committee has primary oversight of the firms approach to ESG, which includes reviewing key ESG-related policies such as our
Environmental Policy Framework and our annual ESG Report.
Other ESG matters are also reviewed by the full Board or its other committees as part of their respective mandates.
