sustainability & environmental, social and governance (ESG) initiatives
We are committed to operating as a sustainable enterprise — socially, economically, environmentally and ethically. Sustainability — especially of the health system — is ingrained in our business strategy, as our products and services deliver intrinsic social value by helping to build a health system that is more affordable and accessible, and that delivers better outcomes for everyone.
Our sustainability commitments and ESG policies and practices are overseen by the Board of Directors’ Governance Committee. Our Chief Sustainability Officer is responsible for developing a comprehensive ESG strategy and long-term sustainability commitments in partnership with our businesses and leaders across the company.
