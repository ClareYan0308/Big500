In the United States and around the world, Occidental continues to be one of the most admired companies in our industry. We are proud to be recognized as a responsible oil and gas and chemicals company and as a Partner of Choice®.
Fortune Most Admired
In 2018, Fortune magazine ranked Occidental No. 1 in the Mining, Crude-Oil Production category in its annual World's Most Admired Companies survey. This was Occidental's 10th time to achieve this top ranking, which we have received every reported year since 2008.
American Chemistry Council Responsible Care® Recognition
Our wholly owned subsidiary OxyChem is a five-time winner of the American Chemistry Council's top safety performance award, including "Responsible Care® Company of the Year" in 2015. These awards recognize OxyChem's outstanding performance in the areas of health, environmental, safety and security, as well as the systems that OxyChem has in place to sustain this performance.
OxyChem's plants have received multiple awards for safety and performance. Learn more here.
Newsweek Green Rankings
Occidental ranked No. 133 in Newsweek magazine's 2017 Green Rankings U.S. 500 list. The ranking assesses the sustainability and environmental performance of the 500 largest publicly traded companies headquartered in the U.S.
Association of Fundraising Professionals Outstanding Large Corporation
The Association of Fundraising Professionals presented Occidental with the Houston Business Journal Outstanding Large Corporation award as part of National Philanthropy Day® in 2017.
