About Marathon Petroleum Corporation
Marathon Petroleum Corporation (NYSE: MPC)
- Third-largest U.S. refiner based on crude oil refining capacity; largest Midwest refiner
- Seven-plant refinery system with approximately 1.8 million barrels per calendar day of capacity
- Petroleum products marketer in the Midwest, Southeast, East Coast, Northeast and Gulf Coast
- Marketing network of approximately than 5,500 Marathon locations and approximately 2,730 Speedway convenience stores
- Including MPLX LP, owns and operates 79 light product and asphalt terminals
- Owns, leases or has ownership interest in approximately 10,800 miles of crude and light product pipelines
- Owns and operates more than 5,600 miles of gas gathering and NGL pipelines
- Company roots reach back to the early years of the oil industry
