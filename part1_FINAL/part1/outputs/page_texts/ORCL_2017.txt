Oracle has acquired Agile, a leading provider of product lifecycle management (PLM) software solutions.
Agile's PLM solutions drive the product innovation and introduction process in a variety of industries, including high-tech, life sciences, industrial manufacturing and consumer packaged goods. Agile's solutions help customers make better product portfolio decisions, collaborate across design and supply chain partners, accelerate new product introduction and manage compliance. The combination of Agile and Oracle's leading enterprise applications will create a best-in-class, integrated enterprise PLM solution.
