Awards & Recognition
We are proud of the recognition achieved throughout our company.
FTSE4Good. In 2016 AbbVie was added to the FTSE4Good index series based on our environmental, social and governance performance
2016 Civic 50. For the third year, AbbVie was recognized as one of America’s most community-minded companies and honored for our commitment to improve quality of life in communities where we do business.
DiversityInc Top 50 Companies for Diversity 2016. For the third consecutive year, DiversityInc recognizes AbbVie as one of the 50 top companies for diversity. This list comprises companies excelling in areas of recruitment, talent development, leadership, commitment and supplier diversity.
Corporate Equality Index. AbbVie has earned a perfect score of 100% on the 2016 Corporate Equality Index (CEI) administered by the Human Rights Campaign Foundation. The Best Places to Work for LGBT Equality recognition underscores AbbVie’s commitment to our LGBT employees and allies. It also reflects our efforts in building a diverse, innovative and open culture that values all employees and their contributions.
2015 Dow Jones Sustainability World Index. For the second consecutive year, AbbVie was named to this global benchmark and recognized for leadership in responsible economic, environmental and social performance.
Working Mother 100 Best Companies. Working Mother magazine has for the third year in a row recognized AbbVie on its annual “100 Best Companies for Working Mothers” list. The list is comprised of companies excelling in workforce representation of women, child care, flexibility programs, leave policies and more.
Calvert Corporate Diversity Practices Report
AbbVie scored 90 out of 100 on the Calvert Corporate Diversity Practices Report. A high score such as this reflects the total embrace and successful implementation of our diverse culture and commitment to continually progress toward a fully inclusive workplace.
Fortune World’s Most Admired Companies 2015. AbbVie ranked seventh in the pharmaceuticals industry in this annual listing, up from eighth in 2014. U.S.-based Fortune magazine develops the annual list by surveying members of the board of directors, executives and financial analysts, who rate companies based on key attributes including innovation, quality of management and quality of products/services.
Corporate Equality Index. AbbVie is pleased to be part of the 2015 Corporate Equality Index (CEI) administered by the Human Rights Campaign Foundation. The positive CEI rating underscores AbbVie’s commitment to our LGBT employees and
allies and reflects our efforts in building a diverse, innovative and open culture that values all employees and their contributions.
2014 Access to Medicine Index (ATMI). AbbVie ranks #9 among the world’s top 20 research-based pharmaceutical companies in ATMI, which assesses how companies ensure products are available, affordable and acceptable to patients. AbbVie was recognized for leadership in research and development, equitable pricing practices, addressing neglected tropical diseases and focus on neonatal health in the Philippines.
AbbVie’s astonishing ascent: Top 20 in Global Brand Strength. AbbVie’s entry into the top 20 of the 2014 FutureBrand Index for global brand strength is considered an unprecedented development for a new company. Our brand, launched at separation from Abbott on January 1, 2013, now shares the “top 20” tier with long established brands like Google, IBM, and Disney in a world-wide survey that ranks the 100 largest market cap companies. Rankings are determined by public perceptions of how a company delivers on its intended purpose. FutureBrand is the world’s leading creative consultancy for future brand expertise.
2014 The Civic 50. For second consecutive year, AbbVie was recognized as one of America’s most community-minded companies and honored for our commitment to improve quality of life in communities where we do business.
DiversityInc Top 50 Companies for Diversity 2014. Ranking among the top businesses that make diversity a key component of management principles, AbbVie’s philanthropy and cross-cultural mentoring program were cited in this annual ranking.
Fortune World’s Most Admired Companies 2014. AbbVie ranked eighth in the pharmaceuticals industry in this annual listing, and ranked fifth in our industry in social responsibility, the use of corporate assets, and quality of products and services categories.
The Civic 50 List 2013. Recognized as one of the most community-minded companies in America, AbbVie was honored for the time, talent and resources dedicated to improve quality of life in communities where we do business.
Institute for Supply Management’s Sustainability Gold Award 2014. AbbVie was the inaugural winner of this award, for a collection of sustainability projects and initiatives submitted by AbbVie’s Environment, Health & Safety (EHS) and Purchasing Supplier Management organizations for accomplishments in Illinois.
2013 Top Employers from Science magazine. AbbVie ranked fourth in our first year on the annual list, with survey respondents ranking AbbVie’s top three driving characteristics as socially responsible, having a clear vision and doing important, quality research.
Barron’s Most Respected Companies 2013. AbbVie was named to this annual list for the first time, ranking 62 among the world’s 100 largest publicly traded companies, as ranked by professional investors and based on characteristics including strong management, sound business strategy and ethical business practices.
Governor’s First Time Recipient Award from the Illinois Sustainable Technology Center (ISTC) 2013. The award recognizes AbbVie’s commitment to environmental excellence and energy efficiency while reducing our environmental footprint in Lake County, Illinois.
2013 Dow Jones Sustainability Index North America. For the first time as a new company, AbbVie was included in this prestigious global benchmark for our leadership in responsible economic, environmental and social performance.
